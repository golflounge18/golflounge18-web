Weekly Round-Of-Golf

Register your junior golfers for six-week sessions.

A round-of-golf every week is the best way for juniors to get the practice in they need.
Each session runs for 6 weeks. Play on Wednesday 3-5pm or Thursday 5-7pm.
Golfers under 10 play at Par-3 courses.

  • Teams of Juniors paired by age and ability
  • Introduce kids to light competition
  • Team Scramble format
  • Stableford scoring

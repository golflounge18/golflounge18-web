Join our weekly Leagues

Leagues for Mens, Womens, Juniors and Seniors.

League rules

Leagues Start in November, and skip the week of Thanksgiving. Leagues will end before Christmas. League runs for 6 weeks, and are designed to allow you to play when you can. Please submit rounds results by closing time on Sunday.

Participate in five rounds of 9 holes per week to qualify for 18 holes Playoffs on the last day of the league. Each weekly league game will be played on different course. (See Course schedule below). If needed, you can bring a sub

Prizes: Apart from bragging rights, and being memorialized as winners of the first league at Golf Lounge 18, the winning team gets 3 hours of Bay time. The runner up team gets 1 hour.

Course Schedule

  • Week of November 05: St. Andrews, Old Course (Scotland)
  • Week of November 12: The Grove (UK)
  • Week of November 19: Innisbrook, Copperhead (Florida)
  • Week of November 26: Skip the week of Thanksgiving
  • Week of December 03: Sea Island, Seaside (Georgia)
  • Week of December 10: Olympic Golf Course (Rio)
  • Week of December 17: Playoffs 18 holes at PGA National, Champion (Florida)

League participation

  • Junior's League on Monday afternoons at 4pm-7pm. Starts on November 5
  • Women's League on Tuesday mornings at 10am-1pm. Starts on November 6
  • Senior's League on Wednesday mornings at 10am-1pm. Starts on November 7
  • Men's League on Wednesday evenings at 6pm-9pm. Starts on November 7
  • Discounted rates for those who play at the preferred times listed above
  • $40 per league game per person: Sign up as an individual (Total $240 per player)
  • $30 per league game per person: Sign up as a group of 4 or more players (Total $180 per player)
  • Payments are due on first day of the league

Note: If you cannot play at preferred times, please reserve your times through our regular reservation system, and play when you can. (Regular bay charges apply.)

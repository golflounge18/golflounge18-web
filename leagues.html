LEAGUE FORMAT AND RULES

  • Leagues available for Mens, Womens, Juniors, Seniors.
  • Starting in the week of January 14
  • Flexible timing: If you cannot play during league times, we will assign you a time that works for you
  • Each team consists of two players.
  • Scoring format: Best ball, handicap-adjusted for Amateur leagues
  • Handicap will be assigned based on scores for the first 3 rounds
  • League will run for 7 weeks
  • Each team will play assigned course every week - 9 holes
  • League dues - 300 per player ($250 for Juniors and Seniors)

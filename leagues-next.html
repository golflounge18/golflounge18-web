GOLF LOUNGE 18 SPRING LEAGUE - STARTING MAR 18 2019

Round up your friends and compete for prizes and bragging rights for the title of League Champions. It's a perfect opportunity to hangout with friends, and meet new ones in a fun and competitive environment.

Register your team for Spring League

Are You Game?

LEAGUE FORMAT AND RULES

  • Starting in the week of March 18
  • Flexible timing: If you cannot play during league times, we will assign you a time that works for you
  • Each team consists of two players.
  • Scoring format: Best ball, index-adjusted for leagues
  • Each team will play assigned course every week - 9 holes
  • League will run for 7 weeks
  • Top 16 teams enter elimination style playoffs
  • Skins: $10, Closest-to-pin $2
  • League dues - 300 per player. ($250 for juniors/seniors)

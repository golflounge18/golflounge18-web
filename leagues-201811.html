Are You Game?

Leagues available for Mens, Womens, Juniors, Seniors.

Duration - Six weeks

Start Date - November 6/7/8, skips Thanksgiving week.

Times - Listed on the Registration form. (If you cannot play at this time, contact us to re-schedule)

Championship play - Week of December 17

League dues - $250 per person ($200 per player for Juniors and Seniors)

LEAGUE FORMAT AND RULES

  • Each team consists of two players. (One sub allowed)
  • Each team will play assigned course every week - 9 holes
  • League will run for 6 weeks
  • Scoring format: best ball
  • Week of December 17 will be Championship week. Teams will compete in 18 hole match play at PGA National Golf Course, Champion (Florida)

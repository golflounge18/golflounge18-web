JUNIOR GOLF AT GOLF LOUNGE 18

Junior programs target golfers of all skill levels through engaging and fun competitions as well as professional instruction. The lessons, camps, leagues, and tournaments hosted in our premium facility, provides a solid foundation for the improvement of fundamentals as well as teach how these players should use these abilities on the course.

Junior Leagues

Golf leagues provide a competitive environment for juniors in a team atmosphere. In groups of two, golfers play nine holes each week in a best-ball format over a variety of simulated courses in locations across the world for prizes and bragging rights. Juniors of all ages can play with friends or meet new ones in this enjoyable and competitive environment.

Family Tournaments

Starting January 2019, Golf Lounge 18 will host a monthly Family friendly tournament, The Adult/Junior Family Tournament. One Adult will pair up with one Junior, and compete in a 9-hole Stableford team scramble tournament.

  • Introduce kids to light competition
  • Team of One Adult, One Junior
  • Team Scramble format
  • Must use two tee shots from the Junior
  • Winners get credit at Golf Lounge

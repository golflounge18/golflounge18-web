Play golf all year round

Weather is no longer an obstacle to playing golf. Play at the most recognized courses in the world, without leaving Fairfield.

  • Book your tee times up to a week in advance.
  • $50 to play a round on peak days (Friday, Saturday Sunday) - includes 20 minutes of practice / warm up, and a round of golf.
  • $35 to play during non-peak days

Memberships

Membership benefits:

  • Book up to 2 weeks in advance
  • 3 rounds of golf during peak days
  • 4 rounds of golf during non-peak days
  • 30 minute lesson with our golf pro
  • 15% off everything else

Membership Cost: $250 per month (Introductory pricing)

Events and Parties

Golf Lounge 18 is the perfect place to have your next event or a get together. We offer up to 4 bays, TVs, speakers throughout the place. We can also provide a golf pro to teach mini-lessons, run contests and games, and address any of your event needs.

We have a variety of packages, including food and drinks. Please fill out this form and our events coordinator will get back to you.

Leagues

Leagues run every Wednesday from 6pm to 8pm

  • Each league runs for 6 weeks.
  • Scoring takes into account only the best 5 of your 6 scores
  • Teams of upto 4 people can participate. If needed, you can bring a sub.

Rewards

  • First place team: 3 rounds of golf
  • Second place: 2 rounds of golf
  • Third place: 1 round of golf

Rates

  • Register your team: $800
  • Sign up as an individual: $250

Open Play

Book golf bays by the hour, for practice or play. Bring up to 4 golfers per bay booking

  • Book up to 1 week in advance
  • Peak hourly rate: $60
  • Off peak hourly rate: $45

Play golf all year round

Weather is no longer an obstacle to playing golf. Play at some of the most widely recognized courses in the world. Always in perfect condition.

Please note: These are rates per bay (not per player). Bring your foursome, and share the cost.

  • Peak: $60 per hour per bay (Friday to Sunday, and all holidays)

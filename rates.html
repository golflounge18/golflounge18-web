Play golf all year round

Weather is no longer an obstacle to playing golf. Play at some of the most widely recognized courses in the world. Always in perfect condition.
Please note: These are rates per bay. Bring your foursome, and share the cost.

  • Off peak hourly rate: $45 (Monday to Thursday)
  • Peak hourly rate: $60 (Friday to Sunday, and all holidays)

Memberships

Membership benefits:

  • Preferential booking for bay reservations
  • 3 free peak hours of golf bay rentals
  • 4 free non-peak hours of golf bay rentals
  • 15% off everything
  • (Optional, Rental fees extra) Lockers - Limited availability - available to members only - coming soon. Do let us know if you would like to rent a locker

Membership Cost: $250 per month

Coming soon: corporate memberships, junior memberships, senior memberships. Do let us know if you are interested

Leagues

Starting in November

Separate weekly leagues available for Mens, Womens, Juniors and Seniors. Click here to signup. Payments at the center on the first day.

  • Each league runs for 6 weeks.
  • Play 9 holes on a different course each week.
  • Scoring takes into account only the best 5 of your 6 scores
  • Teams of upto 4 people can participate. If needed, you can bring a sub.

Rewards:

  • First place: 3x 1 Hour golf bay rental time
  • Second place: 2x 1 Hour golf bay rental time
  • Third place: 1x 1 Hour golf bay rental time

Rates

  • Register your foursome: $200 per player
  • Sign up as an individual: $250

Social, Corporate and Golf Outings

Golf Lounge 18 is the perfect place to have your next event or a get together. We offer up to 4 bays, TVs, speakers throughout the place. We can also provide a golf pro to teach mini-lessons, run contests and games, and address any of your event needs.

We have a variety of packages, including food and drinks, and are happy to work with your budget. Please fill out this form and our events coordinator will get back to you.
